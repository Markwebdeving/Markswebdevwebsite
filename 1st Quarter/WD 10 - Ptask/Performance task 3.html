"Harmony in Cyberspace: A Poem on Proper Internet Use"



In the realm of the web, let's tread with care,
A world of knowledge, vast and rare.
With every click and every share,
Responsibility we must bear.


To connect and learn, we all aspire,
But let's ensure our hearts don't tire.
For kindness, respect, and truth's the fire,
That fuels the internet's great empire.



In this digital age, where voices unite,
Let's use our screens to spread the light.
With empathy as our guiding site,
We'll make the online world pure and bright.


So surg with purpose, and always be wise,
Guard against the web's deceitful guise.
With proper use, our spirits will rise,
A harmonious internet, our ultimate prize.
